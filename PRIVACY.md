# Privacy Policy

**claude-socratic-plugin** does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data.

## What the plugin does

- Reads and writes configuration to `~/.claude/socratic-config.json` on your local machine
- Reads and writes a concept glossary to `~/.claude/socratic-glossary.md` on your local machine
- Injects context into your Claude Code session at startup via a SessionStart hook

## What the plugin does NOT do

- Does not send any data to external servers
- Does not track usage, sessions, or behavior
- Does not include analytics or telemetry of any kind
- Does not access your code, files, or credentials

All data stays on your machine. The plugin is fully open source and auditable at [github.com/chrlss11/claude-socratic-plugin](https://github.com/chrlss11/claude-socratic-plugin).
